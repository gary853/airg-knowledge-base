## 1. Villa del Palmar Cancun Beach Resort & Spa (HOME RESORT)

**Location:** Playa Mujeres, Quintana Roo — 7 mi NE of Cancun, 25 min north of hotel zone, 35 min from CUN airport
**Type:** Family-oriented, all-inclusive and room-only
**Rooms:** 415 (538–4,630 sqft), most with ocean views
**Suite Features:** Kitchens, living/dining areas, washer/dryer, USB outlets, dimmable lights, automated curtains

### Dining (5 Restaurants + 6 Bars)
- La Casona Steak House
- Zama (modern Mexican)
- Hiroshi (sushi)
- Fresh seafood options
- Vibrant buffet — all gourmet dining included in AI plan

### Amenities
- Aqua Park, 5 outdoor pools, Village Spa (Mayan-inspired), fitness center
- Kids club, family-friendly spa treatments, water sports (kayak, paddleboard)

### Nearby (Playa Mujeres)
- 5 min to Isla Mujeres ferry (dolphin swimming, whale shark encounters)
- 15 min to malls, aquarium, Museo Maya, nightlife
- Day trips: Isla Mujeres, Chichen Itza, Tulum
- Gentle waves (buffered by Isla Mujeres)

### Best For: Families, multi-generational groups
### Best Time: Nov–Apr (dry season); avoid Aug–Oct (hurricane)

## 8. Sierra Lago Resort & Spa — Mascota (MOUNTAIN RETREAT)

**Location:** Mascota, Jalisco — 2.5 hrs from PV airport, Sierra Madre mountains
**Type:** Boutique mountain resort, 27 luxury lakeside cabins
**Setting:** Lake Juanacatlan (ancient volcano crater), crystal-clear lake, forest-covered mountains

### Dining: Gourmet farmhouse-style, locally sourced, farm-to-table
### Amenities: Heated infinity pool, jacuzzis, spa, stables (horseback riding), soccer, tennis/basketball
### Activities: Pedal boats, kayaking, horseback riding, bonfires, stargazing (minimal light pollution)

### What Makes It Special
- ONLY mountain property in TAFER portfolio
- Most remote and intimate (27 cabins)
- Unparalleled stargazing (no light pollution)
- Access is difficult (winding 70-mi mountain road from PV) — but universally worth it

### Best For: Nature lovers, couples seeking remote retreat, stargazers, adventure seekers
### Best Time: Nov–Apr (dry season)
