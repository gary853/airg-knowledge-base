## 3. Points System & Usage

### Annual Allocation
- **4,000 points per year**
- Points-based vacation club (not fixed week, not fixed unit)
- Flexible booking across all TAFER/Villa Group properties

### Banking (Saving Unused Points)
- Bank unused points for up to **5 successive calendar years**
- **No fee** if banked before October 31st
- **$70/week fee** if banked after October 31st deadline
- Biennial members: can only bank 1 year forward

### Borrowing (Future Points)
- Borrow points from up to **5 successive future calendar years**
- **No cost** to borrow
- Biennial members: can only borrow 1 year in advance

### Preferred Points
- Usable between **May (week 18) and October (week 43)** only
- Pro-rated maintenance fee applies
- Can request reservations up to 12 months in advance
- **Cannot be rented, banked, or borrowed**

### Reservation Rules
- Book from **24 months to 60 days** before check-in
- Cancel **60+ days before** = full point restoration (cancellation fee may apply)
- Cancel **less than 60 days** = points may be forfeited

## 6. Exchange Programs (RCI & Sabio)

### RCI Exchange Network
- **What it is:** Global timeshare exchange network — trade your TAFER points for stays at 4,300+ resorts worldwide
- **How it works:** Bank your points/weeks with RCI → search for exchanges at other resorts globally
- **Search window:** RCI provides a 2-year window to utilize deposits
- **Tiers available:** Standard RCI, Signature Selection RCI, Registry Collection (Elite members)
- **Important:** Once vacation weeks are "banked" with RCI, they CANNOT be used within TAFER Residence Club — they must be used at an alternate location

### Sabio Membership
- **What it is:** Internal exchange/wholesale travel platform (part of TAFER ecosystem)
- **Benefits:** Net-rate hotels, discounted flights, discounted cruises, book-with-points options, concierge-curated trips
- **Value proposition:** Wholesale pricing — lowers your cost basis for non-TAFER travel
- **SFX-style exchange:** Additional internal trading benefits across the ecosystem
