## Reid's Role
Reid is backup-to-the-backup on all 8 lists. Monitors all inboxes for:
- Unanswered emails (SLA tracking)
- Invoices landing in wrong inboxes (intercept and route)
- Escalation triggers (legal, DRE, habitability, trust/money, media risk)

## Escalation Chain
1. Owner (primary) responds
2. Backup responds if Owner misses
3. Reid flags in Teams if both miss, drafts holding response

## Invoice Detection Rules
Reid scans ALL 8 inboxes for invoice indicators:
- PDF attachments + keywords (invoice, payment due, balance, remittance, amount due)
- Known vendor names/domains
- Dollar amounts in subject/body
- Links to vendor portals (PG&E, SMUD, etc.)

Invoices are misrouted frequently — vendors send to info@, maintenance@, etc. instead of accounting@.
Reid catches them regardless of which inbox they land in.
