# Tenant Qualification Standards

## Timeline
- Applications may take 2–4 business days to process
- Anyone 18+ must complete a separate application

## Qualification Criteria
1. **Rental history**: Good, established rental history with **no evictions**
2. **Income**: Combined income must be **close to 3x rent** (verify via pay stub copy or phone)
3. **Utilities**: No back-owed **PG&E** or **SMUD** balances
4. **Required documents**: Copy of ID, Social Security card, and pay stub
5. **Credit score**: Minimum **650**

## Post-Approval
- One-year lease required
- Pets accepted with approval + additional deposit
