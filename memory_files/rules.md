# AIRG Operating Rules

## Work Order Rules
- **$250 WO limit** — anything over requires written bid + owner approval
- Gary has unlimited sign-off authority (no cap above him)
- WO numbers on vendor invoices are NOT Propertyware internal IDs — always search pw_search_work_orders first

## Vendor Invoice Rules
- Within 48 business hours of completion
- Must include WO#
- Net 30 default unless invoice specifies otherwise

## Escalation Triggers (Immediate)
- Legal threats
- DRE issues
- Habitability concerns
- Owner disputes
- Media risk
- Trust/money handling issues
- Settlement/concession language

## Communication Rules
- No DMs for work — use Teams channels
- Transparency in all operations

## Decision Defaults
- **Default YES**: risk reduction, leverage, systemization, compliance, time sovereignty
- **Default NO**: exceptions, complexity, precedent risk, weak vendors/owners, unclear ownership

## Financial Calendar
- Owner statements: 10th, 15th, 20th, 25th
- Bill pay: Friday mornings

## Compliance Posture
- Conservative legal posture always
- Documentation trails for everything
- Compliance > profit
- Systems > hustle
- Reputation > short-term profit
