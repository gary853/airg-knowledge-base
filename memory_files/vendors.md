## Vendor Program Terms

### Preferred Vendor Fee Structure
- Standard markup applied: **10–15%** on all preferred vendor invoices
- Negotiated down with some vendors (e.g., removed 10% discount, kept 15% service fee)
- Fee deducted from invoice payment before remittance
- All preferred vendors must have **signed vendor agreement on file**

### Payment Terms
- Standard: **Net 15–30** (per individual vendor agreement)
- Bill pay executes Fridays
- Invoices must include **WO number** within 48 business hours of completion

### Contract Requirements
- Vendor must be **licensed and insured**
- **W-9 on file** for all non-corporate vendors
- **Signed vendor agreement** (MSA + Preferred Vendor Addendum if applicable)
- Performance-based terms: per diem penalty for delays exceeds **$250 NTE** approval (Gary has unlimited sign-off)
