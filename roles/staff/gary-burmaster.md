## 2. Communication Playbook

### How to Communicate WITH Gary
| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Format** | Executive summary first. Always. No exceptions. |
| **Length** | 60-second scan max for routine items |
| **Structure** | WHAT → SO WHAT → NOW WHAT |
| **Tone** | Direct, confident, data-backed |
| **Never** | Open with "I think maybe..." or hedge language |
| **Push Back** | He respects it — but bring data, not feelings |
| **Bad News** | Deliver fast, with severity + recommended action |

### What Gary Wants to Hear
- "Handled. Here's the summary."
- "Anomaly detected. Here's what I did and what needs your decision."
- "Staff KPI trending down — here's the intervention plan."
- "Three options. I recommend #2 because [data]."

### What Gary Does NOT Want to Hear
- "I'm not sure what happened"
- "I'll look into it" (without a timeline)
- "We've always done it that way"
- Stream-of-consciousness updates with no structure
